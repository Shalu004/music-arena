Our Story

Unveiling the sound of our Artist's.

Born in the vibrant music scene of India in 2018, Music Arena emerged from a chance encounter at a local music festival. What started as an impromptu jam session between strangers quickly evolved into a powerful musical connection that would change our lives forever.


Our journey has been marked by countless late-night rehearsals, small pub gigs, and a relentless drive to create music that moves people. From our first EP release in 2019 to selling out major venues across India in 2022, every milestone has shaped who we are today.
